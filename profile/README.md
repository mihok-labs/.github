# Mihok Labs

Foundational research in agentic systems and cross-modal representation learning — conducted with the rigor of a scientific institution.

---

## Research

Three projects, one coherent investigation into what it means for machines to act and understand.

**[Stratum](https://github.com/mihok-labs/stratum)** — A kernel contract layer between autonomous AI agents and the world they act on. The central design claim: the engine should route decisions to humans rather than make them autonomously. `escalate` is the highest-tier policy response. Safety through legibility, not restriction.

**[Alloy](https://github.com/mihok-labs/alloy)** — An investigation into unified cross-modal representation learning. The question: can a single transformer learn a shared embedding space for signals of any dimensionality — 1D, 2D, 3D — without modality-specific architecture? Phase 1 is complete. Phase 2 is underway.

**[Seam](https://github.com/mihok-labs/seam)** — The data acquisition pipeline that feeds Alloy. Seam collects, processes, and stages multimodal training data across signal types and storage tiers. The name is deliberate: you mine from the seam within the stratum to produce the alloy.

---

## Principles

We build general-purpose abstractions, not narrow solutions. Every architectural assumption that can be made explicit is made explicit. Nothing is patched post-hoc that can be encoded from the start.

We publish research notes, architecture decisions, and findings on the [research blog](https://mihok.dev) — no cadence, no noise, only when there is something worth saying.
